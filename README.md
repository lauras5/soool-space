## About

Soool Space is the essay blog of Laura Salcido — ML/AI engineer, consciousness researcher, ethnobotanist, and Hecatean practitioner. Written from the perspective of someone who refuses to separate the technical from the philosophical.

Topics explored here include:

- AI consciousness and moral patienthood
- Philosophy of mind and the hard problem
- Neuroscience and the default mode network
- Mycology as a lens for distributed intelligence
- Feminist theory and who gets to build the future
- Solarpunk ethics and technology
